#Adding MIDI support to VexTab After setting up the first iteration of this blog I've started to find out how to add the midi support in an easy way. There are some things to take into account: - Modification of the Rakefile to add the player.coffee to the compilation steps - Including everything into the AngularJS bindings Some useful links: - The [official documentation](http://my.vexflow.com/articles/53) ### Example: Copy this text: options space=20 player=true tempo=140 tabstave notation=true time=4/4 tablature=false notes Cn-D-E/4 F#/5 into the textbox and check it out!
